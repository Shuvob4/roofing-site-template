---
question: "How long does a typical roof last?"
---

The lifespan of a roof depends on the material used. Asphalt shingles typically last 20-30 years, metal roofing can last 40-70 years, and premium materials like slate can exceed 100 years. Regular maintenance and prompt repairs can significantly extend the life of any roof. During your free inspection, we'll assess the remaining life of your current roof and recommend the best options for your budget.

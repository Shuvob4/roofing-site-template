Spring is the ideal time to inspect your roof after the harsh winter months. Our spring inspection checklist includes:

- Check for ice dam damage and lifted shingles
- Inspect flashing around vents, chimneys, and skylights
- Clear debris from gutters and downspouts
- Look for signs of moisture or mold in the attic
- Assess overall shingle condition and granule loss

Schedule your spring inspection early to catch minor issues before they become costly repairs.

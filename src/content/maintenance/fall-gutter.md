Preparing your roof and gutters for winter is essential to prevent ice dams and water damage. Our fall maintenance service includes:

- Complete gutter and downspout cleaning
- Inspect and repair gutter hangers and seams
- Check roof for loose or damaged shingles before winter
- Verify attic ventilation is adequate to prevent ice dams
- Trim overhanging branches that could cause damage in storms

Don't wait until the first snowfall — get your roof winter-ready this fall.
